← back Diffusion-Limited Aggregation

Origin

Witten and Sander, 1981. Produces Brownian tree fractals with dimension approximately 1.71. Paul Bourke reference. Jason Webb experiments.

How It Works

One seed pixel at the center. Random walkers are released from edges. Each walker does a random walk until it touches the existing cluster, then sticks permanently. Results look like lightning bolts, mineral dendrites, frost, and river deltas.

Applications

Models crystal growth, electrodeposition, mineral dendrites, lightning paths, river delta formation, and bacterial colony growth.
