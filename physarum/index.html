Origin

Jeff Jones, Characteristics of Pattern Formation and Evolution in Approximations of Physarum Transport Networks, 2010. Implements his multi-agent model with the four core parameters. Sage Jensen. Bleuje explainer.

How It Works

Follows the Jones 2010 algorithm. Each agent senses trail at three positions offset by the sensor angle and distance. If front is strongest, go straight. If both sides beat front, turn randomly. Otherwise turn toward the stronger side. Agents deposit trail, which undergoes 3x3 mean diffusion and multiplicative decay each step.

Applications

Bio-inspired network optimization and logistics. Physarum algorithms have replicated Tokyo rail networks. Extended by Sage Jensen in 36 Points with parameter-dependent dynamics. Used in procedural art, maze solving, and supply chain design.
