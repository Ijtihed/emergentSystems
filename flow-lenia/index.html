Origin

Plantec et al., Flow-Lenia, 2025. Extends Lenia by Bert Wang-Chak Chan, 2018. A mass-conservative continuous cellular automaton where multiple species coexist, compete, and evolve.

How It Works

Cells hold continuous values from 0 to 1. Each species has a ring-shaped kernel defining its neighborhood and a Gaussian growth function. Three species, alpha, beta, and gamma, compete for space with approximate mass conservation.

Applications

Artificial life and open-ended evolution research. Understanding biological ecosystems and species competition. Generative art producing organic, lifelike animations from simple continuous rules.
