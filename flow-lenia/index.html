Origin

Plantec, Hamon, Etcheverry, Oudeyer, Moulin-Frier, Chan. Flow-Lenia, best paper at ALIFE 2023, published in Artificial Life 2025. Extends Lenia by Bert Wang-Chak Chan, 2018. Won best paper for enabling open-ended evolution in continuous CA.

How It Works

Standard Lenia computes potential via kernel convolution, then applies a Gaussian growth function. Flow-Lenia adds mass conservation by computing gradient flows from the growth field, mass moves from low-growth to high-growth regions rather than being created or destroyed.

Applications

Enables spatially localized patterns that behave like creatures, showing directed motion, chemotaxis, division, and self-repair. The mass conservation makes patterns persistent and allows multi-species ecosystems to emerge from shared dynamics.
